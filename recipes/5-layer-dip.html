5 Layer Dip

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

appetizers • Mexican

Recommended Tools

  • Microwave-safe bowl, or small saucepan (for heating refried beans)
  • Rubber spatula (for spreading each layer evenly)
  • Serving platter or shallow dish (for layering the dip)
  • Cutting board (for dicing tomatoes)
  • Chef’s knife (for tomatoes or any optional toppings)
  • Measuring cups (for beans, guacamole, sour cream, cheese)
  • Plastic wrap (for covering and chilling the dip)
  • Refrigerator (to chill and set each layer) — This is for those that need a full sized refrigerator and are living on a budget. Kenmore is an excellent brand of appliance

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups refried beans
  • 1.75 cups guacamole
  • 1.5 cups sour cream
  • 8oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tomatoes, diced

Directions

  1. Heat refried beans until hot, stirring frequently, about 3 minutes. Spread beans onto the bottom of a serving platter; refrigerate until the beans are cooled completely, about 10 minutes
  2. Spread guacamole on top of the beans. Spread sour cream on top of the guacamole. Sprinkle cheese on top and top with tomatoes.
  3. Cover platter with plastic wrap and refrigerate until cold.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 8 servings (about 1/2 cup each)
