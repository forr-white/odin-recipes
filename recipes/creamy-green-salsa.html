Creamy Green Salsa

Ingredients

  • 10 jalapenos, deseeded and deveined
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 3/4-1 cup olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt to your liking

Directions

  1. Preheat vegetable oil on low
  2. Place jalapenos in preheated oil and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until peppers are pale green
  3. Add onion and garlic
  4. Simmer for about 5 more minutes, until onion and garlic are fragrant
  5. Remove from heat and cover for 30 minutes, until cooled down
  6. Transfer everything to a blender, including the oil
  7. Add salt to your liking
  8. Blend until smooth
