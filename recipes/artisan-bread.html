No-Knead Crusty Artisan Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1-1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1-1/2 cups luke warm water

Directions

  1. Combine the three dry ingredients in a large bowl then pour in the luke warm water. Stir until
    completely combined. Dough will look messy and not smooth
  2. Once combined, cover tightly with plastic wrap and let sit on the counter overnight. Let it rise
    between 12-18 hours. Dough will be puffed up, appear bubbly, and be sticky.
  3. When dough is done rising and ready to bake, preheat oven to 450 degrees
  4. Heavily flour clean counter top or surface. Turn dough onto floured surface and shape into a
    round ball. Cover lightly with plastic wrap and let dough rest
  5. While the dough is resting, place dutch oven into the oven for 30 minutes to pre-heat
  6. After it has preheated, place ball ove dough in the center of the dutch oven and cover with lid
  7. Bake at 450 degrees for 30 minutes, remove lid and bake for an additional 15 minutes until done.
    Bread will be golden in color.
