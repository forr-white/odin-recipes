Royal Icing

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons meringue powder
  • 9-10 tablespoons water
  • optional gel food coloring

Directions

  1. In a large bowl using hand/stand mixer with whisk attachment, beat all of the ingredients
    together on high speed for 1.5-2 minutes. When lifting the whisk up off the icing, the icing
    should drizzle down and smooth out within 5-10 seconds. If it's too thick, add a little more
    water. If it's too thin, add a little more powdered sugar.
  2. Icing completely dries in about 2 hours at room temp. If you need to set quickly, place in
    refrigerator to speed up.
