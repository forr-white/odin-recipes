Copycat Texas Roadhouse Butter

sauce • American

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions

  1. Place butter, confectioner’s sugar, honey, cinnamon, and salt in a deep bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until well combined and slightly fluffy.
