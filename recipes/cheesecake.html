Cheesecake

Ingredients

Ingredients for Crust

  • 2 cups (198 grams) graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup (67 grams) sugar
  • 7 tablespoons (99 grams) butter, melted

Ingredients for Cheesecake

  • 5 blocks of full-fat cream cheese (40 ounces total) room temperature
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (300 grams) granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 5 large eggs room temperature
  • 3 large egg yolks room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (113 grams) heavy cream room temperature

Directions

Directions for Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F). Lightly spray a 9" springform pan with non-stick spray.
  2. Wrap the bottom and sides of the pan with heavy duty tinfoil. I recommend doing several
  3. diligent layers here to ensure no water creeps through when you place the pan in the water bath. Set pan aside.
  4. In a large bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter; stir well to combine.
  5. Firmly pat the mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 8 minutes. Place partially baked crust on a cooling rack
  7. and set aside while you prepare the filling.

Directions for the Cheesecake Filling

  1. In the body of a high power blender, food processor, stand mixer fitted with the whisk
  2. attachment, or in a very large bowl using a hand held mixer, beat the softened cream
  3. cheese until completely smooth, scraping the bowl as needed.
  4. Add sugar and vanilla and beat smooth, scraping down the sides and bottom of bowl as
  5. needed. Add in the eggs and yolks, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Add in the cream and beat until it's just incorporated in the batter.
  7. Pour filling into prepared crust and, using a silicone spatula, smooth the top.
  8. Place the cheesecake pan into a large, deep pan. Fill the pan with 2 inches of hot water.
  9. This is your water bath and will help ensure your cheesecake comes out crack free.
  10. Carefully place the pan in the oven and bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes. Turn oven off and
  11. let the cheesecake sit, undisturbed, for 45 minutes, inside the oven with the door shut.
  12. The cheesecake should be still slightly wiggly.
  13. Remove cake from oven and gently run a knife very around the edge of the cake. Place the
  14. cheesecake pan on a cooling rack and cool completely, then loosely cover the pan with
  15. saran wrap and chill for at least 8 hours.
  16. Cheesecake will keep, covered in the fridge, for 5 days. Cheesecake may be frozen for
  17. 2 months. Thaw overnight before slicing.
