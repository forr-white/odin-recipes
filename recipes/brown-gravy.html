Brown Gravy

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cube beef bouillon, or 1 teaspoon better than bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup cold water + 3 Tablespoon corn starch
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Add the chicken broth, beef broth, beef bouillon, onion and garlic powder, and Worcestershire
    sauce to a medium saucepan. Stir to combine
  2. Combine the cold water and corn starch in a small bowl with a lid. Shake to combine.
  3. Bring gravy to a boil. Whisk in cornstarch mixture. Decrease to medium low heat. continue to
    whisk to ensure that it blends smoothly
  4. Let it gently simmer until its of desired thickness
  5. Remove from heat. Swirl in one tablespoon of cold butter just before serving, to add a silky
    finish.
