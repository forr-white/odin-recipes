Fresh Lime Margarita

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tequila
  • 1/4 cup Triple Sec
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons superfine sugar
  • 4 lime wedges
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1-1/3 cups crushed ice

Directions

  1. In a pitcher, combine the first 5 ingredients, stir until the sugar is dissolved
  2. Moisten rims of 4 margarita glasses with lime wedges.
  3. Sprinkle salt on a plate and dip glass rims in salt.
  4. Serve over crushed ice in prepared glasses.

Optional:

You can add 3/4 cups of cran-watermelon juice and increase tequila to 3/4
cup for a refreshing watermelon-lime margarita
