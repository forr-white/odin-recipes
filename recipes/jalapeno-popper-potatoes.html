Jalapeno Popper Loaded Potatoes

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 large baking potatoes
  • 9 oz bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 6 oz cream cheese
  • 6 oz cheddar, grated
  • 3 fresh jalapenos, deseeded and finely diced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • olive oil
  • sour cream
  • fresh chives

Directions

  1. Stab the potatoes a few times all around. Coat in a drizzle of olive oil and a good pinch
    of salt and pepper. Place in the oven at 350 degrees for 60-70 min, or until tender.
  2. Once cool enough to handle, slice off the top and scoop out most of the center, leaving
    a thin wall to keep the potato sturdy
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the potato filling with cream cheese, garlic powder,
    butter, a good pinch of salt and pepper and most of the cheddar, bacon and jalapenos.
  4. Evenly divide the filling among the shelled potatoes, then top with remaining cheddar,
    bacon and jalapenos. Place back in the oven at 430 degrees for 15 minutes or until the
    cheese turns nice and crispy
  5. Serve with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of chives.
