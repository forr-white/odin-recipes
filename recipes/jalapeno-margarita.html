Jalapeno Margarita

drinks â€¢ Mexican

Ingredients

  • Kosher Salt and Lime Wedges, to rim glass
  • 2 oz silver tequila
  • 3 tbsp fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh squeezed orange juice
  • 1.5-2 tsp light agave nectar
  • 1/4-1/2 jalapeno pepper, cut into thin round slices
  • Lime wedges and additional jalapeno slices for serving

Directions

  1. Rim the glass. Pour a thin layer of salt onto a plate. Rub a lime wedge around the
  2. top edge of your glass to moisten it, then dip the glass into the salt so that it sticks,
  3. tapping all the way around. Fill the glass with ice and set aside.
  4. Fill a small cocktail shaker with additional ice. Add the tequila, lime juice, orange juice,
  5. agave, and jalapeno slices. Tightly close and shake vigorously for 30 seconds. Strain
  6. into rimmed glass. Enjoy immediately, garnished with lime wedges and additional
  7. jalapeno slices
