Instant Pot Crack Chicken

Developed by Cook Anything Kitchen

dinner • American

Named for its "addictive" combination of ranch seasoning, cream cheese, bacon, and cheddar, this shredded chicken is a keto-friendly staple. Using the Instant Pot makes the chicken incredibly tender and easy to shred, while the sauté function allows you to crisp the bacon in the same pot for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

The Proteins

  • 4 slices thick-cut bacon, diced
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp paprika (sweet or smoked)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

The Creamy Ranch Sauce

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp dried dill weed
  • ½ tsp dried chives
  • Note: You can substitute the spices with 1 tbsp store-bought Ranch seasoning mix.

Toppings

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh parsley and sliced scallions

Directions

  1. Crisp Bacon: Turn the Instant Pot to "Saute". Add the diced bacon and cook until it reaches your desired crispness. Remove the bacon with a slotted spoon and set aside, leaving the rendered fat in the pot.
  2. Sear Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add olive oil to the pot if needed. Sear the chicken for about 2 minutes per side until lightly browned. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Deglaze (Important): Press "Cancel". Pour in the chicken broth. Use a wooden spoon to scrape all the browned bits (fond) off the bottom of the pot. This prevents the "Burn" warning.
  4. Prepare Sauce: In a small bowl, mash the softened cream cheese with the ranch spices (garlic, onion, dill, chives).
  5. Pressure Cook: Place the chicken breasts back into the pot with the broth. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the chicken. Secure the lid and set to High Pressure for 12 minutes.
  6. Release: When the timer goes off, perform a Quick Release of the pressure.
  7. Shred: Remove the chicken to a cutting board and shred with two forks. Whisk the liquid remaining in the pot until the cream cheese is fully incorporated and smooth.
  8. Thicken: Add the shredded chicken and shredded cheddar cheese back into the pot. Stir to combine. Close the lid (with the power off) and let it stand for 5 minutes to thicken.
  9. Serve: Stir in the crispy bacon. Serve hot on buns, over rice, or in lettuce wraps. Garnish with parsley and scallions.

Notes

  • The "Burn" Error: Always ensure you scrape the bottom of the pot after sautéing. Any stuck-on bits can prevent the pot from coming to pressure.
  • Low Carb Tip: Serve this in large Romaine lettuce leaves or over riced cauliflower for a perfect keto-friendly meal.
  • Frozen Chicken: You can use frozen chicken breasts, but increase the pressure cook time to 15 minutes and ensure they are not stuck together.
