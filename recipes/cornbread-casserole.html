Cornbread Casserole

Submitted by Cook Anything Kitchen

sides • Southern American

Main Ingredients

  • 2 (8.5 oz) boxes Jiffy-style cornbread mix
  • 2 (14.75 oz) cans creamed corn
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 stick (½ cup) melted butter

Optional Add-Ins

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1 small can diced green chiles
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt (only if needed—mixes vary in saltiness)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9×13 baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together: cornbread mixes, creamed corn, sour cream, eggs, and melted butter until combined.
  3. Fold in optional cheddar or green chiles if using.
  4. Pour into the prepared dish and smooth the top.
  5. Bake 45–55 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.
  6. Rest 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

  • For a firmer, less custardy casserole, reduce sour cream to ¾ cup.
  • For extra sweetness, add 2 tbsp sugar.
  • For a spicy version, add 1–2 diced jalapeños or ½–1 tsp cayenne.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 12 Servings
