Easy, Healthy, Taco Rice Bowl

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

dinner • Mexican Fusion

This bowl is the ultimate meal prep solution. Packed with protein from lean ground turkey and black beans, plus fresh crunch from salad greens, it’s a healthy twist on taco night that doesn’t sacrifice flavor. The secret is the quick cilantro-lime rice that ties everything together.

Ingredients

The Base & Protein

  • 1 lb ground turkey (93% lean recommended)
  • 3 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 2 cups cooked rice (white or brown)
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 2–3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 6 cups salad greens (romaine, spinach, or iceberg), roughly chopped

Fresh Toppings

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Greek yogurt taco sauce (see notes)

Optional Garnishes

  • Salsa or Pico de Gallo
  • Jalapeño slices
  • Tortilla chips (for crunch)
  • Extra lime wedges

Directions

  1. Cook Turkey: Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until browned (about 10–12 minutes). Halfway through cooking, stir in the taco seasoning and a splash of water if needed to coat the meat evenly. Remove from heat.
  2. Prep Rice: While the meat cooks, make the quick cilantro lime rice. In a bowl, combine the hot cooked rice, juice of ½ lime, chopped cilantro, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of black pepper. Fluff with a fork to combine.
  3. Assemble Base: Start with a bed of salad greens in each bowl. Add about ½ cup of the cilantro lime rice on top.
  4. Add Toppings: Top each bowl with 4 oz of the seasoned turkey, ¼ cup black beans, ¼ cup shredded cheese, sliced avocado, and a handful of quartered tomatoes.
  5. Finish: Drizzle with Greek yogurt taco sauce, salsa, or your favorite dressing. Serve with lime wedges and tortilla chips if desired.

Notes

  • DIY Greek Yogurt Sauce: Mix ½ cup plain Greek yogurt with 1 tablespoon taco seasoning and a squeeze of lime juice for a creamy, high-protein dressing.
  • Meal Prep: If prepping for the week, keep the salad greens and avocado separate until ready to eat to prevent sogginess/browning. Warm the turkey and rice before adding to the cold greens.
  • Carb Control: For a lower-carb option, swap the white rice for cauliflower rice.
