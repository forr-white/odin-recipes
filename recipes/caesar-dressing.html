Homemade Caesar Salad Dressing

Ingredient:

  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp anchovy paste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Mix all wet ingredients together with a whisk until well combined
  2. Add in Parmesan cheese, whisk until well combined
  3. Add salt and pepper and mix until combined and enjoy!
