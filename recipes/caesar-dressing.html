Caesar Dressing

sauce • Italian American

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp anchovy paste
  • 2 small or 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ tsp salt and pepper
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano

Directions

  1. Mix all wet ingredients together with a whisk until well combined
  2. Add in Parmesan cheese, whisk until well combined
  3. Add salt and pepper and mix until combined and enjoy!
