Blue Ignition

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

drinks • American

Spark your day with the Blue Ignition. This vibrant, electric-blue cooler combines tart blue raspberry with tropical passion fruit and crisp apple juice. It's a refreshing mocktail on its own, or you can add liquid caffeine drops to turn it into a homemade energy drink without the crash of commercial brands.

  • Highball or Pint Glass (16 oz capacity)
  • Bar Spoon (for stirring gently)
  • Jigger or Measuring Cup (for measuring syrups)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (1 cup) chilled apple juice
  • 6 oz (¾ cup) chilled white grape juice or light lemonade
  • 2 oz (¼ cup) blue raspberry syrup (or blue raspberry drink mix)
  • 1 oz (2 Tbsp) passion-fruit syrup (or passion-fruit drink mix)
  • Liquid Caffeine Drops (optional, see notes)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: splash club soda for fizz
  • Optional: fresh fruit garnish (lime wheel, raspberries)

Directions

  1. Ice: Fill a tall glass (16 oz) about ¾ full with ice.
  2. Base: Pour in the apple juice and white grape (or lemonade) juice.
  3. Flavor: Add the blue raspberry syrup, passion-fruit syrup, and caffeine drops (if using). Stir well to combine.
  4. Fizz (Optional): If you like a little fizz, top with a splash of club soda and stir gently.
  5. Serve: Garnish with a lime wheel or a few raspberries if desired. Serve immediately while chilled.

Notes

  • Caffeine: Use liquid caffeine drops or concentrate for the cleanest flavor and easiest mixing. Most liquid caffeine products contain roughly 100 mg per teaspoon — add 2 teaspoons to reach 200 mg caffeine (similar to a standard energy drink). Always check your specific bottle's concentration.
  • Lower Sugar: To reduce the sugar content, use light apple juice and sugar-free syrups.
