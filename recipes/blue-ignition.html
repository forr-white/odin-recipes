Blue Ignition

Submitted by Cook Anything Kitchen

drinks • American

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (½ cup) chilled apple juice
  • 6 oz (¾ cup) chilled white grape juice or light lemonade
  • 2 oz (¼ cup) blue raspberry syrup (or blue raspberry drink mix)
  • 1 oz (2 Tbsp) passion-fruit syrup (or passion-fruit drink mix)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: splash club soda for fizz
  • Optional: fresh fruit garnish (lime wheel, raspberries)

Directions

  1. Fill a tall glass (16 oz) about ¾ full with ice.
  2. Pour in the apple juice and white grape/lemonade juice.
  3. Add the blue raspberry syrup and passion-fruit syrup; stir to combine.
  4. If you like a little fizz, top with a splash of club soda and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel or a few raspberries (optional).
  6. Serve immediately chilled.

Notes

  • Use liquid caffeine drops or concentrate for the cleanest flavor and easiest mixing.
  • Most liquid caffeine products contain 100 mg per teaspoon — add 2 teaspoons to reach 200 mg caffeine.
