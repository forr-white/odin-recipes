Watermelon Agua Fresca

Ingredients

  • 6 cups ripe watermelon, cut into cubes (seedless preferred)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 cup cubed ice
  • 1 tablespoon sweetener (optional)

Directions

  1. Wash and cut the watermelon into large cubes. You will need 6 heaping cups of watermelon.
  2. Add the water and watermelon to the blender. Blend for about one minute until smooth.
  3. For little to no pulp, use a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to strain the liquid into a pitcher. If using a high-powered blender, straining should not be necessary. If using a watermelon with seeds, definitely strain to get rid of any extra seeds.
  4. Pour the drink into a pitcher or bottle. Add 1 cup of ice and stir. Do not add too much ice as this will dilute the flavor and sweetness.
  5. Place container in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to an hour until cold.
  6. Store any leftover agua fresca in an air-tight bottle or other container for up to 3 days. The pulp will settle at the bottom so give the bottle a quick shake before serving.
