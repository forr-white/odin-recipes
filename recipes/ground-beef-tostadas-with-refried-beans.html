Ground Beef Tostadas with Refried Beans

dinner • Mexican

Ingredients

  • 12 tostada shells
  • 1 large russet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ½ small onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, diced
  • 1 Roma tomato, diced
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cumin
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups refried beans (see notes for instructions)
  • shredded lettuce
  • diced tomatoes
  • sour cream
  • Jalapeño Salsa or Salsa Verde Taquera

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, heat two tablespoons of oil on medium-high.
  2. Add diced potato and sauté until soft on the inside and golden on the outside.
  3. Make a well in the middle of the potatoes and add ground beef, breaking it up with a large spoon as it cooks.
  4. Once browned, add onion and garlic and incorporate with the potatoes.
  5. After one minute, mix in the diced tomato.
  6. Cover and turn off heat.
  7. Prepare refried beans (see notes for details).
  8. Assemble tostadas by spreading an even layer of beans on the tostadas first.
  9. Top with a couple tablespoons of ground beef.
  10. Garnish with lettuce, tomato, sour cream and Jalapeño Salsa or Salsa Verde Taquera.
