Crockpot Hot Chocolate

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

drinks • American

Ingredients

  • 1 giant Hershey’s chocolate bar
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1.5 cups heavy cream
  • 6 cups whole milk
  • 1–2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 scoops Hershey’s cocoa powder
  • Marshmallows, for topping

Instructions

  1. Add the Hershey’s bar (broken into pieces), sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, whole milk, vanilla, and cocoa powder to the crockpot.
  2. Whisk well to combine.
  3. Cook on LOW for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally until smooth, melted, and creamy.
  4. Ladle into mugs and top with marshmallows.
  5. Keep on WARM for serving.

Notes

  • For extra richness, add an additional scoop of cocoa or a handful of chocolate chips.
  • This stays smooth on WARM for several hours—perfect for parties or holidays.
  • Top with whipped cream, crushed peppermint, caramel drizzle, or sprinkles.
  • You can substitute half-and-half for heavy cream for a lighter version.
  • Use an immersion blender before serving for a silky texture.
