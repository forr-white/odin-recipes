Homemade Corn Tortillas

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Maseca Corn Flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 Cups Warm water

Directions

  1. Combine flour and salt
  2. Add water slowly, until it comes together. It will no longer stick to your hands and will be
    smooth in texture
  3. Once dough is smooth, wrap with plastic and allow to rest for 1-2 hours outsidd the refrigerator.
  4. Separate dough into 1-inch rounds
  5. Line tortilla press with 2 pieces of plastic
  6. Place dough ball in center of tortilla press and gently press, flip and gently press again.
  7. Preheat skillet on low-medium heat.
  8. Plasce dough in center of skillet and cook for 30 seconds on the first side, or until you see a slight
    discoloration around the edges.
  9. Flip tortilla and cook for 45 seconds to 1 minute.
  10. Flip again and gently press in the center of the tortilla, it should puff up.
  11. Repeat 6-10 with remaining dough balls.
