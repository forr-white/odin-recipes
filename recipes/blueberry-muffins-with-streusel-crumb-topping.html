Blueberry Muffins with Streusel Crumb Topping

Ingredients

For the Muffins:

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2-1/3 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 stick salted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line standard muffin pan with liners and set aside.
  2. To make the crumb topping: In a small bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and cinnamon, add melted butter and stir
  3. with a fork until crumbly and set aside.
  4. To make the muffins: In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, and salt and set aside.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs and sugar until combined. Whisk in yogurt, oil and vanilla extract.
  6. Fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix everything together by hand.
  7. In a small bowl, sprinkle 1 cup blueberries with 1-2 tablespoons flour and toss them until all blueberries are coated
  8. with thin layer of flour and then fold them gently int the batter
  9. Spoon batter into prepared muffin tins, filling about 2/3 of each cup. Cover the batter with the remaining blueberries
  10. and then cover the blueberries generously with streusel topping.
  11. Place them in the oven and reduce the heat to 375. Bake about 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  12. Cool for 5 minutes in the pan then remove muffins and cool on a wire rack.
