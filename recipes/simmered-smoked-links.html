Simmered Smoked Links

Submitted by Maxine Cenker

snacks • American

Ingredients

  • 2 packages (16 ounces each) miniature smoked sausage links
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup prepared horseradish

Directions

  1. Place sausages in a 3-qt. slow cooker. Combine the brown sugar, ketchup and horseradish; pour over sausages and stir. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.

Notes

  • Optional:
  • 1 small onion, finely diced – caramelizes beautifully in the slow cooker.
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple (drained) – gives it a tangy-sweet tropical lift.
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell pepper – for a little color and crunch.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 Serving, 1 Link

Calories: 46

Fat: 3 g

Carbohydrates: 3 g

Protein: 1 g
