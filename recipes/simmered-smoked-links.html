Simmered Smoked Links

Submitted by Maxine Cenker

appetizer • American

These aren't your average "Little Smokies" swimming in plain BBQ sauce. The secret ingredient here is prepared horseradish. It cuts through the rich sweetness of the brown sugar and ketchup, adding a zesty kick that makes these impossibly addictive. The perfect "set it and forget it" party appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages (16 oz each) miniature smoked sausage links (Lit'l Smokies)
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup prepared horseradish (jarred)

Directions

  1. Assemble: Place the miniature sausages in a 3-qt. slow cooker.
  2. Make Sauce: In a medium bowl, whisk together the brown sugar, ketchup, and prepared horseradish until combined.
  3. Combine: Pour the sauce mixture over the sausages. Stir gently to ensure every link is coated.
  4. Cook: Cover and cook on Low for 3–4 hours. The sauce will thin out as it heats up, then thicken into a glaze.
  5. Serve: Keep the slow cooker on the "Warm" setting and serve with toothpicks nearby.

Notes

Optional Add-ins:

  • Onion: Add 1 small finely diced onion at the beginning; it caramelizes beautifully in the slow cooker.
  • Tropical Twist: Add ½ cup crushed pineapple (drained) for a sweet and tangy variation.
  • Crunch: Add ¼ cup finely chopped bell pepper for color and texture.
