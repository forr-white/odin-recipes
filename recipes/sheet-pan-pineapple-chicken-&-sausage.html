Sheet Pan Pineapple Chicken & Sausage

dinner • American

Ingredients

For the Sheet Pan

  • 2–3 green bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 12–16 oz smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1.5–2 lbs chicken breasts or thighs, cut into chunks
  • 2–3 tbsp olive oil
  • Seasonings:
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp onion powder
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp Italian or Cajun seasoning
  • 1–2 cups pineapple chunks (fresh or canned, drained)

Homemade Sweet & Sour Sauce

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar or white vinegar
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C).
  2. On a large baking sheet, combine bell peppers, onion, sausage, chicken, olive oil, and seasonings. Toss well to coat.
  3. Arrange in an even layer, then add pineapple chunks over the top.
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway. Broil the last 2–3 minutes for browning if desired.
  5. While baking, prepare the sweet & sour sauce: Combine pineapple juice, vinegar, sugar, ketchup, and soy sauce in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the cornstarch slurry and cook until thickened, about 1–2 minutes.
  7. Drizzle the sauce over the hot sheet pan meal or serve on the side.

Notes

  • Chicken thighs stay juicier than chicken breast.
  • Add red or yellow peppers for more color.
  • Add red pepper flakes or sriracha for heat.
  • Broiling at the end gives caramelized edges.
  • Serve over rice for a complete meal.
