Egg White Bites

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1.5 cups Liquid Egg Whites
  • 1 Bell Pepper, Diced
  • 1 cup Kale, Shredded
  • Salt, To taste
  • Pepper, To taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Spray a 12-cup muffin pay with your favorite nonstick spray
  3. You can make these egg bites in a bowl or in a food processor/blender
  4. If using a bowl, add the ricotta and liquid egg whites together and whisk together.
  5. Then fold the bell peppers and kale until evenly distributed
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Transfer to the muffin cups leaving 1/4 inch of space at the top
  8. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes until the edges are golden on the edges and cooked fully through
  9. Let cool slightly and enjoy!
