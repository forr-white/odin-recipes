Creole Succotash from Treme

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 2 ribs celery, chopped
  • 1 pound fresh or frozen okra, trimmed and cut crosswise into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 pound fresh or frozen lima beans
  • 1 pound fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 1 pound smoked pork sausage, cut crosswise into 3/4 inch slices
  • 1 pound boneless smoked ham, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Pinch of dried thyme
  • 1/2 to 1 cup water, if needed
  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Hot cooked rice for serving

Directions

  1. In a large heavy-bottomed saucepan or dutch oven over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the
  2. tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, and celery and stir to coat well in the fat. Cook, uncovered,
  3. stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender, 10 to 15 minutes
  4. Stir in the okra, lima beans, corn, sausage, ham, bay leaves, and thyme. Cook, still uncovered,
  5. until vegetables are soft, about 1 hour, stirring occasionally. If the mixture seems dry toward the
  6. end of the cooking time, stir in water to moisten, a little at a time, then cover the pan and
  7. continue cooking.
  8. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the shrimp. Cook, stirring often, until the shrimp are cooked
  9. through and the flavors have blended, about 15 minutes longer. Remove from the heat. Mound
  10. the rice on large plates, poon the succotash over rice and serve.
