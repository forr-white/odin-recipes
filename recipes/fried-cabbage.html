Fried Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 12oz Bacon, Diced Raw
  • 1 Head Cabbage, Chopped
  • 1 Large White Onion, Diced
  • 2 Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Add chopped bacon to a large pot. Cook over medium heat until extra crispy.
  2. Remove bacon from pot, leaving the grease.
  3. Add cabbage to pot and cook over medium high heat for 10 minutes, stirring every minute or so.
  4. Add onion and continue cooking 5 more minutes, still stirring every minute.
  5. Add Worcestershire, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, and brown sugar.
  6. Reduce heat to medium and continue cooking for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is cooked through.
  7. Add cooked bacon to cabbage and taste to adjust seasoning and serve.
