High Protein Potato Bowls

Home

Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes (approx. 1200g total), cube them up
  • 0 calorie avocado spray for the pan
  • 1 white onion (approx. 150g), diced
  • 2lb (908g) lean ground beef (96/4)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne (adjust for spice level)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 bag mexican cheese

Garlic Herb Sauce

  • 1 cup (240g) non-fat Greek yogurt (or dairy-free option)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Water, as needed to thin the sauce

Directions

  1. Place cubed sweet potatoes in a pre-greased pan and sprinkle with salt. Bake in 400 degree oven for 30 minutes
  2. Sautee onions for 3-5 minutes then add garlic.
  3. Sautee garlic until fragrant, add beef and chop it up. Add remaing spices. Cook until browned
  4. For the Garlic Herb Sauce, add all ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth. Add water to thin out the sauce.
  5. Divide the potatoes and meat into 8 even portions and layer the meat on top of the potatoes. Sprinkle the cheese on top.
