Paloma (Mocktail)

Submitted by Forrest White

drinks • Mexican

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces fresh grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces sparkling water (or grapefruit soda for a sweeter version)
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • ¼ ounce agave nectar or simple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • Coarse sea salt, for the rim
  • Ice
  • Grapefruit wedge or lime slice, for garnish

Directions

  1. Rim your glass with lime juice and dip it in coarse salt.
  2. Fill the glass with ice.
  3. In a shaker or glass, combine grapefruit juice, lime juice, and agave. Stir well.
  4. Pour into the prepared glass and top with sparkling water.
  5. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge or lime slice.

Notes

  • For less sugar, use unsweetened sparkling water and reduce the agave nectar.
  • For extra fizz, swap sparkling water for club soda.
  • You can add a splash of non-alcoholic tequila alternative (like Ritual or Lyre’s) for a more authentic flavor profile.
