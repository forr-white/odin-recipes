Bacon, Cheddar & Monterey Egg Bites

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

breakfast

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • ½ cup cottage cheese (or heavy cream for creamier texture)
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 5 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • Optional: chopped chives or green onions for garnish
  • Optional: a pinch of smoked paprika

Directions

  1. Cook the bacon: Fry or bake bacon until crisp. Let cool, then crumble into small pieces.
  2. Blend the base: In a blender, combine eggs, cottage cheese (or cream), salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Blend until completely smooth — this gives the egg bites that Starbucks-style silky texture.
  3. Prepare your molds: Spray a silicone muffin mold or egg-bite mold generously with nonstick spray. Add a small pinch of bacon, cheddar, and Monterey Jack into each cup.
  4. Fill the molds: Pour the egg mixture over the cheese and bacon, filling each cup about ¾ full. Add a bit more cheese or bacon on top if desired.
  5. Cook – Sous Vide / Instant Pot: Add 1 cup water to your Instant Pot. Place mold on a trivet, cover loosely with foil. Cook on Steam for 10 minutes, then natural release for 10 minutes.
  6. Cook – Oven method: Preheat oven to 300°F. Place the silicone mold on a baking sheet. Bake 28–32 minutes, or until set with a slight jiggle.
  7. Cool & release: Let egg bites rest 5 minutes before removing from molds. Garnish with chives or smoked paprika if desired.

Notes

  • For ultra-creamy bites, use cottage cheese—it’s the secret to the Starbucks-style texture.
  • Try swapping the bacon with diced ham or sausage for variations.
  • These freeze well! Let cool, wrap individually, and freeze up to 2 months. Reheat 45–60 seconds.
