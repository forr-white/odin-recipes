Bacon, Cheddar & Monterey Egg Bites

Submitted by Cook Anything Kitchen

breakfast

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • ½ cup cottage cheese (or heavy cream for creamier texture)
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 5 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • Optional: chopped chives or green onions for garnish
  • Optional: a pinch of smoked paprika

Directions

  1. Instructions
  2. Cook the bacon
  3. Fry or bake bacon until crisp. Let cool, then crumble into small pieces.
  4. Blend the base
  5. In a blender, combine eggs, cottage cheese (or cream), salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  6. Blend until completely smooth — this gives the egg bites that Starbucks-style silky texture.
  7. Prepare your molds
  8. Spray a silicone muffin mold or egg-bite mold generously with nonstick spray.
  9. Add a small pinch of bacon, cheddar, and Monterey Jack into each cup.
  10. Fill the molds
  11. Pour the egg mixture over the cheese and bacon, filling each cup about ¾ full.
  12. Add a bit more cheese or bacon on top if desired.
  13. Cook (Two Options)
  14. Sous Vide / Instant Pot (Most Tender):
  15. Add 1 cup water to your Instant Pot.
  16. Place mold on a trivet, cover loosely with foil.
  17. Cook on Steam for 10 minutes, then natural release for 10 minutes.
  18. Oven Method:
  19. Preheat to 300°F (low heat prevents overcooking).
  20. Place the silicone mold on a baking sheet.
  21. Bake 28–32 minutes, or until set with just a slight jiggle.
  22. Cool & Release
  23. Let egg bites rest 5 minutes before removing from molds.
  24. Garnish with chives or smoked paprika if desired.

Notes

  • For ultra-creamy bites, use cottage cheese—it’s the secret to the Starbucks-style texture.
  • Try swapping the bacon with diced ham or sausage for variations.
  • These freeze well! Let cool, wrap individually, and freeze up to 2 months. Reheat in microwave 45–60 seconds.
