Smashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs yukon gold potatoes
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • olive oil spray
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. In a large stock pot filled halfway with water, bring to boil and liberally salt water.
    Add potatoes, and boil for 20 minutes, or until they are fork tender. Strain; set aside.
  3. Lightly grease a large baking sheet or tray with non-stick spray. Arrange potatoes on the
    sheet and lightly flatten the potatoes with a potato masher or fork in one piece.
  4. Mix together the butter, garlic, and parsley. Pour over each potato. Season with salt and
    pepper to taste.
  5. Place in oven and bake 10-15 minutes or until browned on top. Remove from oven and sprinkle
    with Parmesan cheese. Return to oven until cheese is melted and browned.
