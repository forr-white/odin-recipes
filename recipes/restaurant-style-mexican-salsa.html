Restaurant Style Mexican Salsa

sauce • Mexican

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1-2 fresh Roma tomatoes
  • 1 medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 jalapeños (or serranos for more heat)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin (optional)

Directions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the canned tomatoes, fresh tomatoes, onion, garlic, and jalapeños.
  2. Add cilantro, lime juice, salt, and cumin (if using).
  3. Pulse until the salsa reaches your desired texture (chunky or smooth).
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning—add more lime, salt, or chili for balance.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors develop.
  6. Serve chilled with fresh tortilla chips.

Notes

  1. For a smoky flavor, try roasting the tomatoes, peppers, and garlic before blending. Salsa will keep in the fridge for up to 5 days.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 8 servings (about 1/4 cup each)

Calories: 25

Fat: 0 g

Carbs: 6 g

Protein: 1 g
