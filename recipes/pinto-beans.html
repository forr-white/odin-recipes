Pinto Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Dried Pinto Beans
  • 1 Pound Ground Meat
  • 1 Each Sweet Onions diced
  • 1 Each Green Bell Pepper diced
  • 1 Rib Celery diced
  • 3 Cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 Pound Smoked Sausage
  • 1 Link Andouille Sausage
  • 3 Each Bay Leaves
  • 1 Tablespoon Creole Seasoning
  • 1 Tablespoon Onion Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 2 teaspoon Dried Thyme dried
  • 5 Cups Chicken Broth
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak Pinto Beans in a few cups of water overnight in water, they should double in size
    and be tender beans.
    Dried Pinto Beans
  2. Brown ground meat in a skillet, drain and add to crock pot.
    1 Pound Ground Meat
  3. Chop all vegetables, add to crock pot
    1 Sweet Onions, 1 Green Bell Pepper, 1 Rib Celery, 3 Cloves Garlic
  4. Add remaining ingredients, including chicken broth.
    1 Pound Smoked Sausage, 1 Link Andouille Sausage, 3 Bay Leaves,
    1 Tablespoon Creole Seasoning, 1 Tablespoon Onion Powder,
    1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder, 2 teaspoon Dried Thyme, 5 Cups Chicken Broth
  5. Stir until mixed well.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.
  7. Serve over rice.
    Salt and Pepper to taste
