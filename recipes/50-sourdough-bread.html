50/50 Sourdough Bread

Submitted by Cook Anything Kitchen

bread • Artisan / European

Ingredients

  • 250 g white bread flour
  • 250 g whole wheat flour
  • 250 g water, room temperature
  • 150 g active sourdough starter
  • 10 g salt (any grind)

Directions

  1. Activate Starter: Feed 3–4 hrs before baking until bubbly and floats in water.
  2. Mix Dough: Combine flour, water, and starter until shaggy. Rest 30–45 min (autolyse).
  3. Add Salt & Knead: Incorporate salt and knead until smooth.
  4. Bulk Ferment: 4–6 hrs at 70 °F until jiggly with bubbles.
  5. Shape & Proof: Rest 15–30 min, shape boule/oval, proof 1.5–2 hrs or overnight cold proof.
  6. Bake: 500 °F Dutch oven 20 min covered, then 450 °F uncovered 15–20 min until golden brown.
  7. Cool: 1 hr on rack before slicing.

Notes

  • This recipe utilizes a low (50%) hydration starter.
  • Optional: Perform 2-3 sets of stretch and folds during the first 1-2 hours of bulk fermentation to build strength.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 10 servings, about 1/10th a loaf

Calories: 210

Fat: 1 g

Carbohydrates: 42 g

Protein: 7 g
