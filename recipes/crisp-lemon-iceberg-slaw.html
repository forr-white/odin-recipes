Crisp Lemon Iceberg Slaw

Submitted by Forrest White

salads • American

Ingredients

  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2–3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1–2 tbsp white vinegar
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Remove any damaged outer leaves from the iceberg lettuce.
  2. Shred the head of lettuce finely using a knife or mandoline.
  3. Transfer the shredded lettuce to a large mixing bowl.
  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, white vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the shredded lettuce.
  6. Toss gently until the slaw is evenly coated.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  8. Serve immediately for maximum crunch, or chill for 10–15 minutes.

Notes

  • Extra crunch: Add thinly sliced green onions, shaved radishes, or julienned cucumbers.
  • More tang: Increase the vinegar or add a small splash of apple cider vinegar.
  • Make ahead: Keep the dressing separate until serving to prevent the lettuce from wilting.
  • Upgrade it: Add fresh herbs like dill, parsley, or chives for brightness.

Nutritional Information
