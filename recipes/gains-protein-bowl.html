Ingredients

  • 3lb Ground Beef
  • 300g Pico De Gallo
  • 200g Taco Sauce
  • 100g Cream Cheese
  • 100g Light Sour Cream
  • 1 bag Mexican 4 Cheese Blend
  • 1 package Taco Seasoning
  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1 can Corn, drained
  • 1 can Black Beans, drained and rinsed

Directions

  1. With the taco seasoning, place ground beef in a large skillet and brown until done.
  2. While beef is cooking, prepare 2 cups of your favorite long grained rice.
  3. After beef and rice are finished cooking, mix all ingredients together, serve and enjoy.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 8 servings (about 1 1/2 cups each)

Calories: 540

Fat: 27 g

Carbs: 34 g

Protein: 37 g
