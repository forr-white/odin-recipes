Gains Protein Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 3lb Ground Beef

  • 300g Pico De Gallo

  • 200g Taco Sauce

  • 100g Cream Cheese

  • 100g Light Sour Cream

  • 1 bag Mexican 4 Cheese Blend

  • 1 package Taco Seasoning

  • 2 cups rice

  • 1 can corn, drained

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:

  1. With the taco seasoning, place ground beef in a large skillet and brown until done.

  2. While beef is cooking, prepare 2 cups of your favorite long grained rice.

  3. After beef and rice are done cooking, mix together all remaining ingredients.
