Garlic Mashed Potatoes

sides â€¢ American

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs golden yukon potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 10 whole cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4-1/2 cup whole milk, depends on your desiered thickness
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Place potatoes and garlic in a pot of cold salted water and bring to a boil.
  2. Let them boil until fork tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Once finished, place potatoes and garlic into a ricer and squeeze over a separate container.
  4. Mix in butter, milk, salt and pepper. Serve and enjoy.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 10 servings (about 1 cup each)

Calories: 225

Fat: 9 g

Carbs: 33 g

Protein: 4 g
