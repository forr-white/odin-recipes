General Tso's Chicken

dinner â€¢ Chinese American

Ingredients

For Chicken

  • 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch knob ginger, minced
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • oil for frying

For Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoons vinegar
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 inch knob ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chinese chili flakes
  • 5 dried red cili peppers
  • toasted sesame seeds, for garnish
  • thinly sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

For Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into bite size pieces and put into a large mixing bowl. Add grated/minced ginger, garlic,
  2. shaoxing wine and soy sauce. Toss together and let marinade 1 hour or overnight in the frige.
  3. Add about 2 inches of oil to a heavy frying pot. Bring temperature to 375 degrees.
  4. Whisk together all purpose flour and cornstarch in a mixing bowl.
  5. Toss chicken in mixture until well coated. Shake off the excess flour and lay on baking sheet.
  6. Fry in batches for 3-4 minutes, until golden brown, then lay on a cooling rack and season with salt.

For Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together corn starch and vinegar until no lumps are found.
  2. Heat wok over medium high heat and add enough vegetable oil to coat the pan.
  3. When oil is shimmering, add minced garlic and 4-5 dried red chili peppers. Shake and stir
  4. vigorously, cook until the garlic is barely toasted.
  5. Add shaoxing wine, let cook for about 20 seconds.
  6. Add chinese chili flakes, sugar, chicken stock, and soy sauce. Stir together and bring to a
  7. vigorous boil. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt.
  8. Let boil for about 30 seconds then add cornstarch/vinegar mixture.
  9. When it thickens, remove from heat and toss the chicken in. Mix well to cover all chicken.
  10. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds, Enjoy.
