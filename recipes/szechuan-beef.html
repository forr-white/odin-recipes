Szechuan Beef

dinner • Chinese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef skirt steak
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp sesame seed oil
  • 2 pinches black pepper
  • 2 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp starch (cornstarch)
  • 8 tbsp (½ cup) olive oil
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1½ cups sliced onion
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1½ cups sliced green bell pepper
  • 2 cups chopped celery
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp cooking wine

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine 1 pound of sliced beef skirt steak, 1 teaspoon of salt, 2 teaspoons of sesame seed oil, 2 pinches of black pepper, 2 egg whites, 6 tablespoons of cornstarch, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Mix well until the beef is evenly coated. Let it marinate for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 4 tablespoons of olive oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the marinated beef and stir-fry until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.
  3. In the same wok, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil along with 2 teaspoons of minced ginger and 2 teaspoons of minced garlic. Stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant. Add 1½ cups of sliced onion, 1 cup of chopped carrots, 1½ cups of sliced green bell pepper, 2 cups of chopped celery, and 2 cups of sliced mushrooms. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes, stirring frequently, until the vegetables begin to soften.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of cooking wine and 1 cup of water to the vegetables. Return the cooked beef to the wok and toss everything together. To thicken the sauce, whisk 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water in a small bowl, then slowly pour into the wok while stirring until the sauce reaches the desired thickness.
  5. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your Szechuan Beef – tender, flavorful, and packed with vegetables.

Nutritional Information
