Copycat Raising Cane’s Sauce

sauce • American

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground fresh black pepper

Directions

  1. Stir together mayonnaise, ketchup, Worcestershire, salt, garlic, and pepper in a medium bowl until combined.
  2. If serving immediately, transfer mixture to a small bowl. Alternatively, cover and refrigerate overnight to allow flavors to meld.
