Fluffy Homestyle Pancakes

breakfast • American

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups whole milk (add up to 2 tbsp more if batter is very thick)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter, plus more for the pan
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: Whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add wet ingredients: Add milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla. Whisk just until combined—small lumps are okay.
  3. Rest batter: Let the batter sit for 5 minutes to activate the baking powder.
  4. Heat the griddle: Warm a skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly butter it.
  5. Cook: Scoop ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles appear and edges set, then flip and cook until golden.
  6. Serve: Top with butter, syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.

Notes

  • Add milk if the batter is too thick; add flour if too thin.
  • Fold in whipped egg whites for extra-fluffy pancakes.
  • Add blueberries, chocolate chips, or nuts right after pouring batter on the griddle.
  • Keep pancakes warm in the oven at 200°F while cooking the rest.
