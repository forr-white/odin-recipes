Pinto Beans for Refried Beans

sides â€¢ Mexican

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups dried pinto beans
  • 2 strops thick cut bacon, diced
  • 1/2 J+B salt pork
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tsp SPG
  • 6 cups water

Directions

  1. Rinse beans and place in a crock pot with water. Turn crock pot on high heat. Place the rest of the ingredients in the pot and cook for 5-6 hours.
  2. For refried beans, add cooked beans to a skillet and cook to desired consistency. Add 1-2 tbsp of lard for a crispier texture

Nutrition

Serving Size: 8 servings (about 1 cup each)

Calories: 290

Fat: 10 g

Carbs: 33 g

Protein: 17 g
