Tamarind Agua Fresca

drinks • Mexican

Ingredients

  • 6 cups ice, or as needed
  • 6 cups water
  • ⅓ cup tamarind concentrate
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 6 slices orange
  • 6 slices lime

Directions

  1. Fill six glasses with ice.
  2. Combine water, tamarind concentrate, and sugar in a pitcher; stir until sugar dissolves. Pour over ice and garnish each glass with an orange and lime slice.
