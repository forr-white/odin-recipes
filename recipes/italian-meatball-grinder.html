Italian Meatball Grinder

sandwich • Italian-American

Ingredients

  • 12–15 cooked meatballs (Classic Italian Meatballs recipe)
  • 4 Italian grinder/sub rolls
  • 2–3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped (optional)

Garlic Butter for Toasting

  • 4 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp Italian seasoning
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prep the Rolls: Split the rolls, mix the garlic butter, brush the inside, and broil 1–2 minutes until lightly golden.
  2. Heat the Sauce: Warm the marinara and toss your cooked meatballs in half of the sauce.
  3. Assemble: Add sauce to the bottom of each toasted roll, place 3–4 meatballs, spoon more sauce, sprinkle Parmesan, and add mozzarella.
  4. Broil & Finish: Broil 1–3 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with basil or parsley.

Notes

  • Add sliced provolone under the mozzarella for extra gooeyness.
  • Sautéed peppers and onions make it “Staten Island style.”
  • Toast both sides of the roll for a crispier base.
  • Assemble right before broiling for meal-prep friendly sandwiches.

Nutritional Information
