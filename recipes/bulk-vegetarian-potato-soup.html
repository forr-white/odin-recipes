Bulk Vegetarian Potato Soup

Submitted by Cook Anything Kitchen

soups • American

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter (or olive oil for vegan)
  • 3 large yellow onions, diced (about 3 lbs)
  • 4 large carrots, diced (about 1½ lbs)
  • 6 stalks celery, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 lbs russet or Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and diced into 1" cubes
  • 14 cups vegetable broth (or enough to just cover potatoes)
  • 2 cups milk (or unsweetened plant-based milk)
  • 2 cups heavy cream or half-and-half (or coconut cream for vegan)
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar (optional, omit for vegan)
  • ½ cup chopped fresh parsley or chives for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large stockpot (at least 12 quarts), melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 8–10 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic, thyme, parsley, and smoked paprika; cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add potatoes, bay leaves, and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20–25 minutes until potatoes are tender.
  5. Remove bay leaves. Using an immersion blender, puree part of the soup (about half) for a creamy but chunky texture.
  6. Stir in milk and cream (or plant-based alternatives). Simmer another 5 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Stir in cheese if using.
  8. Garnish with parsley or chives before serving.

Notes

  • Use inexpensive ingredients like potatoes, carrots, and onions — skip cream and use water or broth powder to stretch portions without losing comfort or flavor.
  • Keep it simple: soft textures make it easy to eat and digest, even without teeth or utensils.
  • Stays filling and warm for hours; reheat with added water if thickened, and serve with bread or crackers for a complete, hearty meal.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 16 Servings (1.5 cups each)

Calories: 220

Fat: 8 g

Carbohydrates: 32 g

Protein: 5 g
