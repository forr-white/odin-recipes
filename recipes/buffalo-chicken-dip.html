Buffalo Chicken Dip

appetizers â€¢ American

Ingredients

  • 2-8oz packages cream cheese
  • 2 cups blue cheese crumbles
  • 2 chicken breasts, poached and shredded
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Franks buffalo wing sauce, used to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Heat cream cheese in microwave until easily spreadable
  3. Blend in blue cheese, chicken, shredded cheddar, and Franks buffalo wing sauce
  4. Sprinkle additional cheddar cheese on top
  5. Bake for 30-45 minutes, until brown on top

Nutrition

Serving Size: 8 servings (about 1/2 cup each)

Calories: 410

Fat: 33 g

Carbs: 3 g

Protein: 25 g
