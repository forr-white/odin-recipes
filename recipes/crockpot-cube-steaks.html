Crockpot Cube Steaks

Developed by Forrest White & Cook Anything Kitchen

dinner • American

This is the ultimate "set it and forget it" comfort meal. Cube steak—an inexpensive cut that has been tenderized—can sometimes be tough if cooked quickly. However, when slow-cooked in this rich, savory onion and mushroom gravy, it transforms into melt-in-your-mouth tenderness that pairs perfectly with mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cube steak
  • 1 packet brown gravy mix
  • 1 packet Lipton onion soup mix
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of celery soup
  • ½ cup water

Directions

  1. Layer Meat: Add the cube steaks to the bottom of the crockpot.
  2. Season: Sprinkle the brown gravy mix and the onion soup mix evenly over the meat.
  3. Add Veggies: Place the sliced onions on top of the seasoned meat.
  4. Add Liquid: Spoon in the cream of mushroom soup and cream of celery soup. Pour in the ½ cup water. (You can gently spread the soup around, but you don't need to mix it thoroughly; it will come together as it cooks).
  5. Cook: Cover and cook on LOW for 6–7 hours (or HIGH for 3–4 hours), until the cube steaks are fork-tender.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with the gravy spooned generously over the top.

Notes

  • Serving Suggestions: This dish screams for mashed potatoes to soak up the gravy. It also works beautifully over egg noodles or white rice.
  • Veggie Sides: Balance the richness with green beans, roasted broccoli, or sautéed zucchini.
  • Thicker Gravy: If the gravy is too thin for your liking at the end, leave the lid off for the last 20–30 minutes of cooking to let some liquid evaporate.
  • Add-ins: Feel free to add 8 oz of sliced fresh mushrooms along with the onions for extra umami flavor.
