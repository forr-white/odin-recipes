Crockpot Cube Steaks

dinner • American

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cube steak
  • 1 packet brown gravy mix
  • 1 packet Lipton onion soup mix
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can cream of celery soup
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Add cube steaks to the bottom of the crockpot.
  2. Sprinkle the brown gravy mix and onion soup mix over the meat.
  3. Add sliced onions on top.
  4. Spoon in the cream of mushroom and cream of celery soups.
  5. Pour in the 1/2 cup water.
  6. Cover and cook on LOW for 6 hours, or until cube steaks are fork-tender.
  7. Serve with the gravy spooned over the top.

Notes

  • Great served with mashed potatoes — the gravy fits perfectly.
  • Rice or buttered egg noodles also work beautifully.
  • For a veggie side, try green beans, roasted broccoli, or sautéed zucchini.
  • If you want a thicker gravy, leave the lid off for the last 20–30 minutes.
  • You can add sliced mushrooms for extra flavor.
