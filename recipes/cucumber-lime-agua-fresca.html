Cucumber Lime Agua Fresca

drinks • Mexican

Ingredients

  • 2 medium cucumbers regular or English
  • ⅓ cup lime juice
  • ¼ cup honey, agave syrup or preferred sweetener plus more per personal taste
  • 6 cups cold water

Directions

  1. Partially peel the cucumbers (see notes). Cut them into large pieces and place them in a blender.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, taste and add more sweetener as needed.
  4. For little to no pulp, use a fine mesh sieve to strain the liquid into a pitcher. If using a high-powered blender, straining should not be necessary.
  5. Serve chilled or over ice.
  6. Store in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days in a tightly sealed jar or bottle. Shake or mix before serving.
