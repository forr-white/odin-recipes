Cucumber Lime Agua Fresca

Developed by Cook Anything Kitchen

drinks • Mexican

Agua Fresca (fresh water) is a staple in Mexican cuisine. Unlike a smoothie, it is light and juice-like. This cucumber version is arguably the most refreshing of them all. It balances the cool, crisp flavor of cucumber with bright tart lime and just enough sweetness to make it addictive.

Recommended Tools

  • High-Speed Blender (Essential for liquefying the cucumber)
  • Fine Mesh Sieve (To strain out the pulp for a smooth texture)
  • Vegetable Peeler (To prevent bitterness from the skins)

Ingredients

  • 2 medium cucumbers (regular or English)
  • ⅓ cup lime juice (freshly squeezed is best)
  • ¼ cup honey, agave syrup, or sweetener of choice
  • 6 cups cold water
  • Extra sweetener to taste (optional)
  • Ice and cucumber slices for serving

Directions

  1. Prep the Cucumbers: If using regular waxy cucumbers, peel them completely. If using English/Persian cucumbers, you can leave some skin on for color. Cut them into large chunks.
  2. Combine: Add the cucumber chunks, lime juice, honey (or agave), and about 2 cups of the water to the blender. (Adding all 6 cups at once might overflow the blender; you can add the rest later).
  3. Blend: Blend on high speed until the mixture is completely smooth and liquified.
  4. Strain (Recommended): For a smooth drink, pour the liquid through a fine-mesh sieve into a large pitcher. Use a spoon to press down on the pulp to extract all the juice. Discard the dry pulp.
  5. Finish: Stir in the remaining water. Taste and add more sweetener or lime juice if desired.
  6. Serve: Pour into glasses over plenty of ice. Garnish with a cucumber slice or lime wheel.

Notes

  • Peeling: If using regular garden cucumbers (which have thick, bitter, waxy skins), peel them completely. If using English or Persian cucumbers, leaving the skin on adds a vibrant green color.
  • Sweetener: Agave syrup dissolves easier in cold water than honey. If using honey, you may want to dissolve it in a splash of warm water before adding it to the blender.
  • Fizz Upgrade: For a sparkling mocktail, fill the glass halfway with the agua fresca concentrate and top with sparkling water or club soda.
  • Storage: Keep in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days. The solids will settle to the bottom, so shake well before serving.
