Soft Sugar Cookies with Royal Icing

Home

Ingredients

  • 2-1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4-1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • Royal Icing

Directions

  1. Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl using a hand or stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat the butter
    and sugar together on high speed until completely smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes. Add the
    egg, vanilla, and almond extract and beat on high speed until combined, about 1 minute. Scrape
    down the sides and up the bottom of the bowl and beat again as needed to combine.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix on low until combined. Dough will be
    relatively soft. If the dough seems too soft and sticky for rolling, add 1 more tablespoon of flour.
  4. Divide the dough into 2 equal parts. Place each portion onto a piece of lightly floured parchment
    paper or a lightly floured silicone baking mat. With a lightly foured rolling pin, roll the dough out
    to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use more flour if the dough seems too sticky. The rolled out dough
    can be any shape, as long as it is evenly 1/4 inch thick.
  5. Lightly dust one of the rolled out doughs with flour. Place a piece of parchment on top. Place the
    second rolled out dough on top. Cover with plastic wrap or aluminum foil, then refrigerate for at
    least 1-2 hours and up to 2 days.
  6. Once chilled, preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line 2-3 large baking sheets with parchment paper or
    silicone baking mats. Carefully remove the top dough piece from the refrigerator. If it's sticking
    to the bottom, run your hand under it to help remove it. Using a cookie cutter, cut the dough into
    shapes. Re-roll the remaining dough and continue cutting until all is used. Repeat with 2nd peice of
    dough.
  7. Arrange cookies on baking sheet 3 inches apart. Bake for 11-12 minutes or until lightly browned
    around the edges.
  8. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes; transfer to a wire rack to finish cooling
  9. Enjoy cookies immediately or wait until the icing sets to serve.
