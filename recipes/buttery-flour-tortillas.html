Buttery Flour Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 3 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Cup Water

Directions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Melt butter in water and stir
  3. Pour butter/water mixture into dry mixture and combine until you for a smooth ball
  4. Cover with plastic for 10-15 minutes
  5. Separate into even sized pieces
  6. Transfer pieces back into bowl and cover for 10-15 minutes
  7. On a lightly floured work surface, roll out your pieces until you have a 10-12" round
  8. Preheat pan over medium heat and put in tortilla
  9. Once bubbles start to form, flip for another 30 seconds
