Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup yellow onion, finely diced
  • 1 rib celery, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup chiken broth
  • 1 cup Brown Gravy
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 3 cups mashed potatoes

Directions

  1. Prepare the potatoes
  2. Prepare the filling
    1. Preheat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the diced onions and celery. Saute for 3
      minutes. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    2. Increas heat to high. Add the ground beef. Cook and crumble until cooked through. Drain all
      excess grease.
    3. Reduce heat to medium-high. Sprinkle in the flour and toss to coat. Add the tomato paste and
      thyme. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
    4. Deglaze the pan with the Chicken Broth. Use a silicone spatula to scrape up any brown bits from
      the pan.
    5. Add half of the brown gravy. Stir in the vegetables until heated through.
    6. Season with salt and pepper and add more gravy if desired. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
      Remove from heat.
  3. Assemble
    1. Transfer to a lightly greased 9 inch pan or 7x11 casserole dish. Carefully spread warm mashed
      potatoes on top.
    2. Use a fork to "rake" lines into the mashed potatoes
  4. Broil
    1. Set the oven rack about 5 inches away from the broiler. Set to a high broil, 550 degrees.
    2. Broil for about 5 minutes, watch after 2. It will go from perfectly browned to burned quickly
    3. Remove from heat and serve.
